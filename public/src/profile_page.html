San Ignacio Care for the Poor Supplemental Feeding

Organized by XU Social Development Office
May 29, 2022 from 9:00 AM to 12:00PM
Ramonal Village

Nazareth, Cagayan de Oro, 9000 Misamis Oriental
Co-organized with XU Central Student Government and XU Office of Student Affairs
The initiative directly responds to Thrust 2 of the Renewed Province Plan of Philippine Jesuit Society.

The chosen beneficiary community for this activity is the informal and vulnerable settlers in Ramonal Village in Brgy Nazareth, specifically the children.

Food you can donate (good for 200 people):

  • Whole fruits and vegetables
  • Ready-to-eat-cooked meals
  • Breads and pastries without perishable filling
  • Canned foods
  • Instant foods
  • Prepackaged dry goods

Food donation drop off site:

Corrales Ave., Cagayan de Oro, 9000 Misamis Oriental

Volunteers needed:

  • 10 XU students
