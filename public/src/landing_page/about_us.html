Our Story

Team Hiraya are members of the Google DSC Xavier participating in the Solutions Challenge. Inspired to solve the pag-pag issue in the PH and the difficult engagement to NGOs, Hiraya has decided to create Bilin.

We believe that everyone has the spirit to give or donate a portion of what you have, Its just that people find it a hastle connect to the right people. It should’nt be that way. Team Hiraya dreams to start a culture to the commonuity where donatiaon are more often. We aim to also break the stigma of throwing surplus foods and that surplus food is still good food and could feed the hungry.

Bilin and Its Ambition

In the Philippines, 1,717 metric tons of food is wasted each day, the Food and Nutrition Research Institute of the Department of Science and Technology said. Likewise, the International Rice Research Institute said P23 million worth of rice is wasted daily, enough to feed 4.3 million individuals. The UN Environment Programme (UNEP) said that 931 million tons of food sold – 17 percent of total food available in 2019 – went into waste bins of households, retailers, and food establishments.
